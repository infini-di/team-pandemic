About




His story...

Michael Tirgardoon is a new singer-songwriter and guitar player based in Boston, MA. His music is a soulful mixture of soft rock/pop with some blues guitar elements.

Michael Tirgardoon started his music career when he was 17 while living in the island of Oahu, Hawaii. He learned to play guitar, bass, and drums when he was in high school and eventually joined a local band. He moved to Boston to attend college and has been living there since.

His music career as a solo artist took shape only a few years ago. He started performing at open-mic nights playing covers by some of his favorite artists such as Incubus, Bruno Mars, and Jimmy Hendrix. He is currently working on writing and recording new original songs and once he is done, he hopes to have a website ready to share his new music with the world.
